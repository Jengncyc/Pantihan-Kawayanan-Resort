HISTORY OF PANTIHAN KAWAYANAN RESORT

The Cabadin family makes their living by cultivating bamboo, which once covered much of the area around the Pantihan Kawayanan resort. The name "Pantihan Kawayanan Resort" derives from the fact that the vehicle that can be seen in front of it or in the emblem is the first jeep in the entire pantihan to be laden with bamboos.
